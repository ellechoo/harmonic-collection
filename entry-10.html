Collection 9 - Totes

Fail Forward Tote:
(2024)
I designed the image on this tote bag as an assignment for my Core 1 Typography class. I ordered this small custom tote online.

I-pad:
I planned out the design (and it’s digital animations) on my I-pad with Procreate.
Haerin Tote:
(2024)
I bought this bag from the Line Store in Times Square. Line was collaborating with New Jeans and this bag was one of the many options available from the merchandise. Haerin is one of my biases from NWJNS and I love this art-style so I couldn't help but pull out my wallet when I saw this bag.

Binky Bong:
New Jeans Official Lightstick. I had hoped I could bring this to one of their concerts if they ever came to NYC but at the time of writing this… they just left ADOR/HYBE (good for them) so the group's future is uncertain. I hope they get to continue their activities in the future!
Parsons Tote:
(2023)
I received this bag after I committed to Parsons School of Design after graduating high school. During my first year, I often used this bag to carry my laptop and supplies to class.

Laptop:
My Macbook Pro. Due to the nature of my major, I could not live without this computer.
German Tote:
(2023)
One of my hometown friends went on a school trip to Germany and she bought me this pretty tote bag as a souvenir. Out of all of my totes, this one texturally feels the most handmade. (I think it is?) I currently use this bag to carry my knitting supplies for my elective.

Knit Gloves:
These are fingerless gloves I made for my knitting elective. I pulled two all-nighters in a row to finish it because I am a slow knitter. They turned out pretty cute but the process was a drag.
APG Tote:

Tennis Racket:
This is my tennis racket. I played in middle school but dropped it because of circumstances during high school. I’ve picked it up again since the start of my second year at college.
